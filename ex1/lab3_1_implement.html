Healthy Lifestyle

A healthy lifestyle means doing things that make you simply happy and feel good. For one person, that may mean walking a mile five times a week, eating fast food once a week, and spending virtual or in-person time with loved ones every other day.

Eat white meat, eat lots of green vegetables and fruits

According to research, white meat such as fish, chicken, meat from seafood is very good for health and less likely to cause cancer than red meat such as beef and buffalo. In daily meals should eat just enough amount of meat the body needs, eat a lot of green vegetables in a meal should eat more than one vegetable such as spinach, carrots, kohlrabi, chayote, etc. eat a lot of fruit.

Exercise regularly

Exercise is a very effective method to lead a healthy life. Sports help us repel diseases, stay healthy, help dispel stress and fatigue, moreover, have a beautiful, balanced body without excess fat.

Drink enough water every day

Water can help flush out excess waste and help reduce bloating. It can ensure the flow of energy and nutrition to different muscles. Water enhances exercise routine, especially in summer.

Sleep well and sleep for 8 hours a day to stay healthy

Sleep is the fuel that helps the body's organs function properly. When you get enough sleep, your body is rested and re-energized after every day of activity. Therefore, reasonable rest and enough sleep is an extremely necessary medicine for health.
Good habits

Building a life of harmony between work and pleasure

Instead of thinking that your work is over and your personal life has just begun, find a way to combine elements of both. Focus on making consistent, healthy, positive choices that reflect your values, responsibilities, and goals in all aspects of your life.

Have a full breakfast

There is a saying: "Eat breakfast like a king, lunch like a queen and dinner like a beggar ", because breakfast should be considered the most important meal of the day, providing energy for the day. after a long night of rest.

Plan a new day

Every morning you should spend 5 minutes to plan your work for the new day. This is extremely important and decisive for a productive working day. This helps you to do everything you do during the day much more smoothly and proactively.
